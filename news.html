How to Get Accommodation in Kigali

Kigali, the capital city of Rwanda, offers a variety of accommodation options to suit every traveler's needs. Whether you are visiting for business or leisure, here are some tips on finding the perfect place to stay:

1. Explore Nyarugenge District

Nyarugenge is one of Kigali's oldest and most historic districts. It offers a mix of budget-friendly guesthouses and mid-range hotels. The area is well-connected to major attractions and public transportation hubs, making it a convenient choice for tourists.

2. Discover Kicukiro District

Kicukiro is known for its serene and residential neighborhoods. This district provides a range of modern apartments, serviced apartments, and guesthouses. It's an excellent option for travelers seeking a quieter atmosphere away from the city center.

3. Experience Gasabo District

Gasabo is home to Kigali's central business district and offers various upscale hotels and boutique accommodations. If you prefer a luxurious stay with access to shopping and dining, Gasabo is the ideal location for you.

Popular Party Spots

Kigali is also known for its vibrant nightlife and entertainment. Here are some popular party spots:

Nyamirambo

Nyamirambo is a lively neighborhood filled with bars, clubs, and live music venues. It's a great place to experience the local music and dance culture.

Kimironko

Kimironko hosts various bars and lounges where you can unwind and enjoy a relaxing evening with friends.

Remera

Remera is another hot spot for nightlife, featuring a mix of trendy clubs and restaurants, perfect for a night of fun and entertainment.

Wherever you choose to stay in Kigali, you will undoubtedly experience the warmth and hospitality of Rwanda's capital city.
